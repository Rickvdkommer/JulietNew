About

Rooted in fashion.
Built on strategy.

I am a strategic brand and marketing consultant working at the intersection of fashion, culture, and commercial clarity. With over a decade embedded inside luxury houses and independent labels, I help brands find their voice, hold their ground, and grow with intention.

Case Studies 01

Maison Elara

A contemporary ready-to-wear label with strong product instincts and a positioning problem. They were priced for luxury, presented as accessible, and losing ground at every touchpoint in between.

Over twelve months we rebuilt the brand from the inside out: refined the visual language, restructured the wholesale narrative, and redefined the seasonal story from collection brief to buyer presentation. The label now commands the shelf space — and the price point — it always deserved.

Wholesale accounts doubled  ·  18 months

3 new European stockists  ·  first season post-reposition

02

Osei Collective

A debut collection with a clear point of view and no infrastructure to carry it. The founders had the vision. They needed the framework, the language, and the relationships to make a first season count.

We built the go-to-market strategy from scratch, directed the press and editorial launch, developed buyer-facing materials, and led outreach across independent retail partners in the UK, Netherlands, and Japan. Twenty-two stockists confirmed before the collection shipped.

22 stockists confirmed  ·  debut season

Press coverage in 6 publications  ·  launch month

03

Studio Voss

A design-led label three seasons in, growing faster than its marketing infrastructure could support. No internal strategy lead, no consistent brand voice, no framework connecting collection to campaign to consumer.

Embedded as fractional CMO across three consecutive seasons, I built the brand framework, architected the content calendar, led a full visual identity refresh, and managed the agency relationships — all without disrupting the production rhythm the founders had worked hard to protect.

3 seasons  ·  fractional engagement

Brand refresh launched without a single delayed drop
